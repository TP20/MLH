About Us



Who we are

Inequality in food distribution is hardly a new topic. There has always been some who have less in the face of others having more. We don't want things to remain this way and we need your help to change it.

As a non-for-profit food rescue organization, we here at Maple Leaf Harvest dedicate ourselves to finding long-term innovative solutions to the issue of food inequality and hunger in Canada.

Many food-related organizations often have food on hand that they don't need; that's where Maple Leaf Harvest comes in. Deploying Health-Department-Certified drivers and refrigerated trucks, we pick up the food and deliver it immediately for free to local soup kitchens and shelters to feed the increasing number of hungry men, women and children at no cost to our donors and beneficiaries.

We accomplish this by looking into previously untapped food sources, using a “just-in-time” delivery model, taking advantage of new technologies and being much more flexible than traditional organizations.

Find out more about What Makes Us Different!

This is a proven concept. Our cost efficient approach comes from a long line of successes across the world but we need your help to achieve our goals in Canada.

80% of every donated dollar goes to our program costs: ”fueling" our transportation system with trained commercial drivers, refrigerated trucks, fuel, phones, and insurance.

The food is available. The people are in need. Maple Leaf Harvest is the solution.
