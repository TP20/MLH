What Makes Us Different

No warehouses = Lower costs
We don’t have warehouses. Since we don’t store the food, we are able to keep our costs down and deliver our donations to those who really need it. Our proven system which is pioneered by Helen verDuin Palit (You should Google her!) for 35 years in the USA allows each meal delivered to cost only $0.25.

MLH Application boosts up efficiency
Our mobile app is a flawless tool that will allow us to further develop our community, our operations and our performance. We are dedicated to helping the hungry and have made an effort to integrate our organization with the technological changes of today's world.

New food sources can surprise you
We build relations and work with previously untapped markets that traditional institutions dealing with poverty often overlook. We go everywhere and work with every institution that may have leftover food that instead of being wasted can be put into better use.

Enjoy our flexibility
We are able to run 24/7, a task that majority of organizations aren’t able to do. We operate and deliver on late evenings as well as weekends.

Fresh and on-time
Using this just-in-time delivery model allows us to expand the types of food we can donate to ones not regularly seen by those who truly need it.
