Kacper Nowacki

Director of Administration and Development
k.nowacki@mapleleafharvest.org

At Maple Leaf Harvest responsible for the general development of the entire organization. Searches for the new opportunities for the organization to grow and deliver better quality of services. Is responsible for establishing partnerships with food donors and emergency feeding agencies. Ensures legal compliance of all the organization’s activities at state and federal levels.

Coordinates preparation of new marketing materials and social media activities. Creates and implements organization’s fundraising and grant development strategy - develops sponsorship proposals, creates grant applications, searches for new revenue sources. Develops and manages timelines for crowdfunding campaigns.

Supervises the preparation of and reviews forms, policies and procedures which guide the staff and volunteers. He is behind volunteer hiring, training and coordination.

Organizing and overseeing the process of developing and implementing MLH App.

In 2010 Kacper joined Centre for Citizenship Education in Warsaw, Poland as a program coordinator, where he worked on several national and international educational projects. These diverse programs include Project “Click”, with Google Poland, “Evening with Ombudsman”,the EU Commission, the Polish Ombudsman Bureau and “Culture & Education in Libraries”. Kacper additionally worked with Academy for the Development of Philanthropy in Poland and was a part of the Networking European Citizenship Education (NECE) initiative. He is also assisting the Gifted People Services - Canadian non-for-profit organization working with children with developmental disabilities.
