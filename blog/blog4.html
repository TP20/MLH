Take the New Year's Challenge

December 9th, 2014

Join our new Crowdfunding Campaign and help us raise $15,000 to start our operations in Ontario. Together we can improve the lives of thousands of people who can move forward to a brighter future without having to worry about their next meal. With your support Maple Leaf Harvevst aims to deliver at least 50,000 pounds of food monthly. The campaign will run from December 10 to December 31, 2014. To find out more visit our campaign's profile.

It is estimated that over 40% of available food goes to waste. Over 800,000 Canadians are accessing food banks monthly, with more than one-third being children. No one can deny that the current need is overwhelming. We believe that the goal of reducing hunger is very achievable. We just need the will to do it. Support our campaign this holiday season by donating today.
