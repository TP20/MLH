Mission Statement

September 1st, 2014

As a non-profit organization, Maple Leaf Harvest is dedicated to providing help where possible to the hungry. We do this by finding otherwise untapped sources of leftover food and delivering it to those who need it the most. Our Mission Statement and Vision reflect our desire to help improve the lives of many Canadians. Mission Statement “Maple Leaf Harvest is a network directly linking, in a responsible and cost effective manner, food donated from companies to emergency food agencies serving the hungry and homeless in Southern Ontario. Maple Leaf Harvest disseminates it’s methodology to encourage creation of Harvest programs across Canada.” Vision “To provide for all hungry people by using all the abundance of food that is currently being wasted.”
